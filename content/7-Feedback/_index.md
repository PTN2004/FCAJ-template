### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The environment at AWS Vietnam is very professional, modern, and friendly. The workspace is comfortable, which helps me focus better, and everyone is open and ready to help when I face difficulties.

**2. Support from Mentor & Admin Team**  
The mentors are very enthusiastic, giving detailed guidance on the livecap project and helping me solve problems on my own. The admin team also quickly assisted with administrative procedures and provided all necessary documents.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The livecap project (developing a Vietnamese-English meeting caption app) is highly practical and directly related to my Cloud Computing major. I got to work directly with services like VPC, EC2, S3, RDS, and CloudWatch, which I only learned in theory before.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
I learned a lot of practical AWS skills using both Console and CLI, understood system design better, and improved my teamwork, communication, and reporting skills.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
Everyone on the team is friendly and respects one another. The atmosphere is comfortable, with no big gap between interns and full-time staff. We always support each other.

**6. Internship Policies & Benefits**  
The internship support is good, and the flexible working hours made it easy for me to manage my school schedule. Internal tech sharing sessions were also very helpful.


### Additional Questions

* **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
  Getting to work on the real-world livecap project on AWS and learning from experienced mentors.
* **What do you think the program should improve for future interns?**  
  It would be great to have short weekly workshops where different teams can share their project experiences.
* **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why?**  
  Definitely. It helps students gain practical experience quickly and learn useful skills for their future careers.


### Suggestions & Expectations

* **Suggestions:** Add some team-bonding activities so that interns can connect more with each other.
* **Expectations:** I hope to have opportunities to continue working or join future projects at AWS Vietnam.
